# Two Separate Frontends

## Plan
1. Scaffold **`frontend-worker`** — Full gig worker experience
2. Scaffold **`frontend-admin`** — Full admin control panel

### `frontend-worker` Pages
- **Login / Signup** — Firebase Auth
- **Home Dashboard** — Coverage card, live trigger stats, earnings protected, risk score badge
- **Claims Page** — History table, claim status tracker
- **Policy Page** — Weekly premium breakdown, coverage details, simulate event button

### `frontend-admin` Pages
- **Admin Login** — Separate admin auth
- **Analytics Dashboard** — KPI cards, Recharts line/bar charts, loss ratio
- **Claims Manager** — All claims table with trust score + fraud badge
- **Zone Risk Map** — High-risk zone cards
- **Fraud Monitor** — Flagged claims list

Both apps: dark mode, glassmorphism, micro-animations, Inter font, premium design.
