"Protecting Gig Workers, One Shift at a Time"

Why Parametric Guard?

Instant Payouts

No tedious claims process. If weather data hits the trigger threshold in your zone, payouts process automatically within 24 hours.

Micro-Premiums

Affordable weekly deductibles starting at ₹29. Pay only for the shifts you work, specifically tailored to your delivery zones.

Fraud Protection

Our proprietary Trust Engine verifies device IPs and historical claims to ensure payouts go to legitimate workers in affected areas.

How It Works

1

Select Coverage

Gig workers choose a customized micro-premium plan based on the zones they typically deliver in.

2

Monitor Triggers

The platform monitors real-time weather and AQI APIs for those specific geographic zones.

3

Automatic Claim

If conditions cross the trigger threshold (e.g. Heavy Rain > 10mm), an automated claim is filed.

4

Receive Payout

Once the Trust Engine validates the worker's presence, the payout is deposited directly.
